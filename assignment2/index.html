Learn about our coffee

As stated above we speacalise in making our own coffee beans below are our avaliable options for this season:

  • Sunrise Burst

    • Notes: A lively blend with vibrant citrus notes and a touch of tropical fruit. This light roast has a refreshing acidity and a clean finish, perfect for a cheerful start to your day.

  • Dawn Patrol

    • Notes: A bold, robust blend with rich nutty undertones and a hint of vanilla. This energizing coffee delivers a full-bodied flavor with a slight sweetness, making it the perfect companion for early risers.

  • Nutshell Harmony

    • Notes: A medium roast that balances roasted almond and hazelnut flavors with subtle caramel sweetness. Its creamy texture and mild bitterness create a comforting, well-rounded cup.

  • Morning Mojo

    • Notes: A smooth medium roast with flavors of caramel and toffee, complemented by hints of dark chocolate. It provides a comforting sweetness and a balanced brightness that energizes your morning routine.
